Our Causes

You can make a difference in a childs life by making a donation.

Back-A-Buddy Campaign

We are raising funds to provide the most vulnerable community members with food vouchers for these challenging times. With winter having arrived along with severe cold fronts, the need for a warm meal and a full belly is even more important.
Donate Now

COVID-19 Emergency

Coronavirus causes illness ranging from the common flu, to more severe respiratory diseases. Donations received through this cause will help us make sure that vulnerable children have the health, education and nutrition support that they need.
Donate Now

Blooming Daisies

We invite people and businesses from all over the world to get involved financially on an once off or monthly basis. The aim of this campaigns is to raise funds for our Social Worker's salary, and therapy for the children to assist them in their difficulties.
Donate Now

Want To Volunteer?

Become a temporary house parent by helping the children with their homework or extra-curricular activities, or assist us by promoting our cause, fundraising or helping with maintenance around our safe-houses.
