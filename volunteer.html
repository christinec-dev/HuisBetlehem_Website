Volunteer

Help the children with Homework, Activities and Lessons.

Lend a hand by becoming a temporary carer for the children at our houses. Activities can include helping them with Maths & Science, Reading, Projects, Recreational Activities and more.

To find out more about our volunteering times and activities, contact Mariaan Lourens at 079 490 5095.

Lend a hand with our Fundraising

If we have an upcoming event that is needed to raise funds for a specific cause, you can come and volunteer to sell tickets, assist at the event and assist during the event planning.

To find out more about our volunteering times and activities, contact Mariaan Lourens at 079 490 5095.
